2018 Rock N Roll Hall of Fame Trivia


Each year, The Rock & Roll Hall of Fame Foundation's nominating committee seelcts the group of artists nominated in the performer category.

Ballots are sent to more than 900 historians, members of the music industry and artists-including every living Rock Hall inductee.

The five performers with the most votes that year become that year's induction class.
